## Subchapter 4a  
**Catarrh.** One who has running at the nose or a cold will be helped by smelling the plants [a-toch-ietl](A-toch-ietl.md) and [tzonpilihuiz-xihuitl](Tzon-pilihuiz-xihuitl.md), andthe catarrh thus cured.
